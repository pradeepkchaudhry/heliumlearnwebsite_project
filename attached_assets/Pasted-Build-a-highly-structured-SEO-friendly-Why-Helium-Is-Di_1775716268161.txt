Build a highly structured, SEO-friendly “Why Helium Is Different” section for the HeliumLearn website.

Business goal:
Show that Helium is not just another EdTech platform that only completes syllabus. Helium focuses on 360° student success through personalization, real feedback, AI behaviour analysis, structured learning strategy, monitored progress, time management, and rewards.

Core message:
Most EdTech platforms sell content and classes. Helium solves the real problems behind low performance:
- lack of personalization
- lack of real feedback
- weak time management
- poor learning strategy
- no visible reward system
- no monitored improvement process

What Helium does differently:
- personalized learning content
- real mentorship + feedback from expert teachers
- AI Behaviour Analysis that identifies mistakes, weak areas, and improvement patterns
- structured learning strategy
- monitored learning process
- time management support
- progress-based recognition and rewards
- student-first 360° improvement instead of syllabus-only teaching

Required section structure:
1. Hero block
   - H1: “Helium understands how students actually improve”
   - Short subheading explaining that Helium is built around student growth, not just course completion
   - Primary CTA: Download App
   - Secondary CTA: Book Free Counselling

2. Problem cards
   Create 6 cards explaining student pain points:
   - Need personalization
   - Need real feedback
   - Mistakes in time management
   - Need learning strategy
   - Need rewards on achievements
   - Need a clear process for getting results

3. Solution cards
   Match each problem with a Helium solution:
   - Personalized content
   - Weekly mentorship + expert feedback
   - AI Behaviour Analysis
   - Structured learning strategy
   - Recognition / progress rewards
   - Monitored improvement process

4. “How Helium Works” infographic
   Create a 5-step visual flow:
   Diagnose → Personalize → Practice → Review → Improve
   Each step should have a short explanation.

5. Difference section
   Include a side-by-side comparison:
   - Traditional EdTech: syllabus completion, one-size-fits-all, weak feedback
   - Helium: personalised learning, monitored progress, behaviour insights, improvement system

6. Trust and proof block
   Add icons or micro-statements for:
   - expert faculty
   - Helium App
   - AI Behaviour Analysis
   - weekly mentorship
   - test series
   - outcomes focus

Design requirements:
- premium startup-style UI
- clean cards
- strong spacing
- mobile-first responsive layout
- visually simple enough for students to understand in 5 seconds
- add infographic feeling with arrows, progress steps, or connected nodes
- use subtle AI feel: glowing lines, data-flow visuals, progress indicators, but keep it readable
- add hover states and soft shadows
- avoid clutter

SEO requirements:
- Use semantic HTML headings
- Include keywords naturally:
  Helium App, AI Behaviour Analysis, personalised learning, weekly mentorship, expert teachers, test series, online coaching, IIT-JEE, NEET-UG, foundation classes
- Add alt text for any illustrations/icons
- Make copy indexable and easy to understand
- Write short, clear subheadings with searchable intent

Tone:
- student-friendly
- trustworthy
- modern
- motivational
- not overhyped
- clear and conversion-focused

Important rules:
- Do not claim unverified results
- Do not use fake statistics
- Do not make it look like generic AI marketing
- Focus on how Helium solves real student problems step by step
- Keep the message: Helium is a 360° learning system, not just a syllabus delivery platform

Output:
Create the full section with reusable components and polished copy.
